"Story First"

My background in film & video production lead me to develop web projects from what I call a "story first" approach. I think not only of your or your company's story but of the user visiting your site. Why is she there? What interaction do you want to see?


This approach makes your blog, your magazine, your online store or community site more than just a brochure. It makes it an experience. Convinced? Let's work together.
